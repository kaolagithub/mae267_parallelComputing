BASIC COMMANDS FOR SLURM JOB SCHEDULING SYSTEM

see: http://www.arc.ox.ac.uk/content/slurm-job-scheduler#SLURMcommands

sacct: report job accounting information about active or completed jobs
scancel: cancel a pending or running job
sinfo: reports the state of partitions and nodes managed by Slurm
squeue: reports the state of jobs
srun: used to submit a job for execution in real time
